PROBLEM SOLVING TO HEALING

Problem solving is often driven by a desire to find solutions and overcome obstacles. It requires analytical thinking, resourcefulness, and determination. While this approach is essential for navigating the complexities of life, it sometimes focuses solely on external fixes, neglecting the internal landscapes where healing truly takes root. As we embrace healing, we cultivate resilience, deepen our capacity for empathy, and open ourselves to a richer, more authentic existence.
